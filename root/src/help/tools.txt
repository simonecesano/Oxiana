Bookmarklet
-----------

Our bookmarklet allows you to quickly add a point of interest to your current map from Google maps. 

It's simple:

- drag and drop the
<a href='javascript:(function(){var%20w%20=%20window.open("");%20w.location.href%20=%20"http://oxiana.herokuapp.com/poi/add?url="%20+%20unescape(encodeURIComponent(window.location.href))})()'>Add to Oxiana</a> bookmarklet to your toolbar 
- search for a place on Google maps
- make sure that you choose only one of the results
- click on the bookmarklet on your toolbar
- done!
